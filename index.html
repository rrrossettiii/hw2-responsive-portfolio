About Me

I moved to NYC nearly 4 years ago to pursue a job as a bartender at a hight end cocktail bar and leave my hometown of San Clemente, CA. After 3 years of honing my craft at a "speakeasy" bar in the lower east side called Figure 19, Covid-19 struck leaving me jobless. I then decided to use my newfound free time to go back to class and elevate my already substantial tech knowledge.

I've been infatuated by technology from a very young age. At the age of 3 I set up my VCR just so i could watch "Jungle Book." At age 9 I taught myself how to solder and began working with various circuts and wiring. Finally at the age of 14 i built my first computer. Now I've begun my journey into the coding sector with the cumulation of my tech knowledge.
